Welcome to the iNaturalist Engagement Reporting Platform!

This platform, developed by the University of Florida’s Global Ecology Research Group, provides visual insights into engagement activity on iNaturalist. Engagement is tracked in two primary ways:

  1. Identifications – insights into how users contribute by identifying observations made by others.
  2. Bioblitz Participation – insights into programming efforts that facilitate engagement through organized iNaturalist projects (e.g., bioblitzes).

How to Use the Platform:

  • For Identification Insights:
    Select the “Identifications” option and enter your iNaturalist username. The platform will generate a report showing your total number of identifications, the number of people you've engaged with, and a timeline of your identification activity. Note: Graphics may take time depending on your activity level.
  • For Bioblitz Insights:
    Select the “Bioblitz” option and enter the relevant project ID. The resulting report will show how the bioblitz contributed to observations and species in a specific place. Note: Optimized for place-based bioblitzes.
